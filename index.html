About Us

It started with a passion for the game of golf.

That passion led to scholarships for some of the Loop founders to play golf competitively at a collegiate level. As poor college students, we found different jobs that allowed us to be around the game we love as well as make some side cash. Over the course of our collegiate career and after having done all the odd jobs the golf industry has to offer, we found the most rewarding job to be that of a caddie. It is not only a fun way to make a living, it takes the entire golf experience to another level.

In the 1960’s the golf cart was created and over time, unfortunately, the role of a golf caddie has slowly diminished at most golf courses. The reason being that most golf courses simply cannot afford the infrastructure and manpower needed to effectively run a caddyshack on a day to day basis. Our vision here at Loop is to bring the caddie back to every golf course in a cost efficient, timely manner… because every golfer deserves a caddie.
